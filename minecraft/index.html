Factions

The server has a number of factions. If you'd like to, you can also make your own.

Starting a Faction

The requirements for a faction are:

1. Must have at least one banner design.

2. Must have a main theme color.

Your faction can be a coalition, which will allow other factions to become members.

A list of all the factions can be found here.
